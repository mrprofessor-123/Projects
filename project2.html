Srinagar: Heaven on earth

Srinagar, the summer capital of Jammu and Kashmir, is a city steeped in history and cultural significance. Founded in the 6th century during the Gonanda dynasty, Srinagar has served as the capital under various Hindu dynasties and was a major center of learning. The city witnessed significant expansions during the 14th-16th centuries, particularly under the Shah Mir dynasty, and emerged as a spiritual center attracting Sufi preachers.Today, Srinagar is a popular tourist destination known for its natural beauty, houseboats, and traditional handicrafts like Kashmir shawls, papier-mâché, and wood carving. The city's rich history and cultural diversity make it a fascinating place to explore and experience the unique blend of "Kashmiriyat"
